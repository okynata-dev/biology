What a Luom is made of.

Grown from a seed

Each token's number is the only input. It decides the body plan, the spines, the stain, the interior — the same seed always grows the same organism, on any screen.

Rendered live, not filmed

What you see is computed in your browser as you watch: real refraction through glass, iridescence, and a slow six-second breath that loops seamlessly.

Rarity you can see

Rank isn't a hidden number. It shows up in the body — extra shells, a crown of spines, a lattice skeleton, orbiting satellites. A Mythic reads as one across the room.
